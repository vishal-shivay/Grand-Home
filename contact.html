Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a quote?
Simply fill in the contact form above or email us directly at Grandhomeconstructionltd@gmail.com. We'll get back to you within 24 hours to arrange a free, no-obligation site visit and consultation.
How long do projects typically take?
Project timelines vary depending on scope. A bathroom renovation typically takes 1–2 weeks, a kitchen 2–3 weeks, a loft conversion 6–10 weeks, and a new build 6–12 months. We provide a detailed schedule in your quote.
Are you fully insured and certified?
Yes, absolutely. Grand Home Construction LTD is fully insured with comprehensive public liability coverage. All our electricians and gas engineers hold the relevant professional certifications required by UK law.
Do you handle planning permission?
Yes. For projects that require planning permission — such as extensions, loft conversions, and new builds — we manage the full planning application process on your behalf, liaising with the local authority throughout.
What areas do you cover?
We are based in London and serve all London boroughs as well as surrounding areas including Essex, Surrey, Hertfordshire, and Kent. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific location.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfer, credit/debit card, and staged payment plans for larger projects. We never ask for full payment upfront — a typical structure is a deposit to commence, with staged payments throughout, and final balance on completion.
